Recipe for Salmon

Description

Salmon is a type of fish that is known for its pink flesh and rich flavor. It is a popular choice for seafood lovers and can be prepared in a variety of ways, including grilling, baking, and pan-searing.

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillets
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon
  • Garlic
  • Herbs and spices

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C)
  2. Season the salmon fillets with salt, pepper, and herbs
  3. Place the salmon on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil
  4. Bake the salmon for 12-15 minutes, or until cooked through
