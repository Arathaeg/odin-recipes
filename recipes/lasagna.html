Recipe for Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a Italian pasta dish consisting out of several layers. The layers usually consist out of pasta sheets, ground meat, tomato sauce, vegetables and cheese. After having been in the oven, the lasagna is cut in square or rectangular portions

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ground meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Vegetables
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Boil the lasagna sheets
  3. Cook the ground meat
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Put the lasagna in the oven for 30 minutes
